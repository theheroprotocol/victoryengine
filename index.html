About Hero Protocol

The hero protocol will be the Ummahs first unification layer. Inspired by Ethereum, the protocol will be a non-profit foundation that oversees a large decentralized treasury built on-top of Ethereum. The foundation will identify, create and champion projects that are too important or wide-reaching to be owned or controlled by any one group.

We aim to create a new paradigm that eliminates the “effort fragmentation” that is so common in our communities, and also eliminate the single point of failure that is seen when a influential muslim figure dies, or is assassinated. The hero protocol is currently being stewarded by a previous member of the Ethereum Foundation and a PhD holder in Quantum Mechanics.

Roadmap

The two major aspects of the hero protcol are:

1) A large ETH treasury built from “stake-donations.” When someone makes a stake-donation (e.g., $1,000 in ETH), their ETH isn’t spent, but instead staked on the network to generate yield. Donors can withdraw their original ETH at any time—the treasury only uses the staking rewards (such as 2% annual yield) for funding initiatives.


For example, Octant (an ETH treasury) has 100,000 ETH staked at current prices ($3,700/ETH), earning 2% yield—about $750,000 per month, while donors’ principal remains available for withdrawal. A treasury like this cannot be shut down through goverment control of banks.


2) A foundation that identifies and allocates to causes that are too important or wide-reaching to be owned or controlled by any one group.


As of August 2025, both of these initiatives are being worked on via our Research Forum.
